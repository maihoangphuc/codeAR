Augmented reality types

AR types

Image Tracking

When a 2D images is found by the camera, it's possible to show some kind of content on top of it, or near it. The content can be a 2D image, a GIF, a 3D model (also animated) and a 2D video too. Cases of use: Augmented Art, learning (Augmented books), Augmented flyers, advertising, etc.

Image Tracking

Location Based

This AR type uses real world locations to display Augmented Reality content, on user devices. Users can move (ideally outdoors) and through their smartphones, they can see AR content in real-world locations.

Location Based

Video Tracking

Like image tracking, this AR type is replaced by mp4 video when you scan the camera into 2D images and you will see your desired video. This helps us watch a movie you love.
